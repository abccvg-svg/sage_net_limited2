About Sage Net

Wholesale equipment, built to be forgotten — because it never fails.

Sage Net Limited was founded in 2022 in Central, Hong Kong, with a simple premise: property management teams shouldn't have to think twice about the safety equipment they rely on every day. We supply traffic cones, belt barriers and related access equipment at wholesale volume, direct to management companies and institutions.

Today we work with all of Hong Kong's Big Four property management groups as well as a growing roster of schools and universities, supplying equipment for car parks, lobbies, construction perimeters and campus events.

We keep the range focused and the fulfilment fast — because the job of this equipment is to hold up quietly, site after site, year after year.
